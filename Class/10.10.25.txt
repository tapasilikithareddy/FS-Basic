> Image: 
    Syntax:
        <img src="filename.ext" alt="Image Name">
            src - Source
            alt - Alternate

> Attribute: It adds additional functionality to tag.
    Syntax:
        <tagName attribute1="value1" attribute2="value2" attribute3=value3  attributeN=valueN>Content</tagName>

> Anchor: Navigate from one web page to another web page.
    Syntax:
        <a href="filename.html">Content</a>
            href - Hyper Reference

> Favicon: (Needs to added in head tag of html file)
    <link rel="icon" href="filename.ext">

> Title: (Needs to added in head tag of html file)
    Syntax:
    <title>Content</title>

> Button: 
    Syntax:
        <button>Content</button>

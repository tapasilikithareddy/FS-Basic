Padding: Used to apply empty space around the content.

    Syntax:

        padding: 10px;

        padding: top-bottom left-right;

        padding: top right bottom left;

        padding-left: 10px;

        padding-right: 10px;

        padding-top: 10px;

        padding-bottom: 10px;

Margin: Applies empty around the border.

    Syntax:

        margin: 10px;

        margin: top-bottom left-right;

        margin: top right bottom left;

        margin-left: 10px;

        margin-right: 10px;

        margin-top: 10px;

        margin-bottom: 10px;

Height of Tag: margin-top + border-top + padding-top + height + padding-bottom + border-bottom + margin-bottom

Width of Tag: margin-left + border-left + padding-left + width + padding-right + border-right + margin-right

Outline: Applies border around the border:

    Syntax:

        outline: outline-width outline-style outline-color;

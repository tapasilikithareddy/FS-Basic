Background Properties:
1. background-color:
2. background-image:
3. background-size: Specify the size of the background.
4. background-position: Specify the position of the background.
5. background-attachment: Decide whether to fix or scroll background along with the content.
6. background-repeat: Controls the repeation of background image

2. background-image:

    Syntax:

        background-image: url('filename.ext');

3. background-size:

    Syntax:

        background-size: 50px | 50% | cover;

4. background-position:

    Syntax:

        background-position: center center | top center | bottom center | top left | bottom right | top center | bottom center;

5. background-attachment:

    Syntax:

        background-attachment: fixed | scroll;

6. background-repeat:

    Syntax:

        background-repeat: no-repeat | repeat;

Font Family:

    Syntax:

        font-family: Arial | Times News Roman;

Box Shadow: Applies shadow around the box.

    Syntax:

        box-shadow: horizontal-offset vertical-offset blur-radius spread-radius color;

Text Shadow: Applies shadow around the text.

    Syntax:

        text-shadow: horizontal-offset vertical-offset blur-radius color;

Overflow: Used to control the overflowed content.

    Syntax:

        overflow: scroll | scroll-x | scroll-y | hidden | auto | visible;
